Frequently Asked Questions

What are the key steps in the legal process for a civil lawsuit?
The legal process for a civil lawsuit typically involves steps such as filing a complaint, serving the defendant, discovery, negotiation, trial, and judgment. Our AI can provide guidance on each stage of the process.



What should I do if I can't afford legal representation?
If you can't afford a lawyer, there are options such as legal aid, pro bono services, and self-help resources. Our AI can help you explore these options and guide you on how to represent yourself if necessary.



What should I do if I'm facing a legal issue outside my country?
Legal matters across borders can be complex. Our AI can provide guidance on international legal issues, including finding international lawyers and understanding relevant treaties and laws.
